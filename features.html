Prediction engine

A tendency board that thinks like a defensive coordinator.

Every active concept gets a score built from your film data, live game plays, situational patterns, sequence history, and star-player impact. Scores normalize into percentages your staff can act on immediately.

  • Exact situation matching — same down, distance, and field zone carry the most weight
  • Live game data builds influence as the game goes on
  • Sequence awareness — what does this offense run after that play?
  • Confidence label tells you how much to trust the board
  • Defensive answer board uses your own system — fronts, coverages, pressures you actually carry

Box + sideline sync

Box coach logs it. Sideline sees it. Instantly.

Box Mode gives the press box coach full detail — situation selectors, post-play logging, analytics, and reports. Sideline Mode strips it down to what the field coach needs in three seconds between plays.

  • Change the down or distance in the box — sideline updates in under a second
  • Sideline coach can also tap down/distance directly if needed
  • Tap Run, Pass, RPO, or Screen on the sideline to log accountability
  • Works on any device — phone, tablet, laptop — no app install
  • Falls back to local mode when stadium WiFi is bad, syncs when signal returns

Film import

Your Hudl chart. Straight into the engine.

Drop any CSV export from Hudl, Excel, or your own charting system. The app auto-detects columns in any order and lets you fix the mapping before anything gets imported. No reformatting required.

  • Auto-detects down, distance, formation, concept, personnel, and more
  • Column mapping corrections before import — nothing locked in until you confirm
  • Save mapping templates per opponent so future imports take seconds
  • Manual film entry if you prefer to enter plays one at a time
  • Import warnings flag skipped rows so nothing is lost silently

Defensive answer board

Recommendations built from your system, not a generic playbook.

Set up your defensive system once in Setup — base defense, run fit, coverage family, pressure style, fronts, and checks. The answer board uses your actual calls, not suggestions from a playbook your staff doesn't run.

  • Front and fit recommendation based on run/pass lean and top concept
  • Coverage check suggestion based on shot alert or screen/RPO tendency
  • Pressure recommendation with a reminder that staff rules override the board
  • Preset systems for 4-2-5, 3-3-5, 4-3, and 3-4

Star player factor

Know how their best player changes what they run.

Chart whether a key offensive player is on or off the field. The engine calculates how their presence actually shifts concept usage, run rate, screen and RPO tendency, red zone involvement, and third-and-long behavior — from your data, not guesswork.

  • ON vs OFF split calculated from real film and live play history
  • Touch concepts — mark which plays are designed for that player
  • Confidence shrinks automatically when sample size is too small
  • Alert shown on the sideline board when the star is on or off the field
  • Supports RB, QB, WR, TE, and Athlete positions
