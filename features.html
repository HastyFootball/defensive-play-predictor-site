Features

Football analytics made coach friendly.

The product is designed to feel like a premium football analytics platform, but focused on what defensive coaches need most: offensive tendency prediction.

AI play prediction

Ranks likely offensive concepts by situation, formation, personnel, game flow, and live tendencies.

Coach-proof import

Import charted CSV data or enter plays manually. No raw film upload is promised.

Sideline mode

Big, tablet-friendly screen for a defensive coordinator during games.

Star-player alerts

Shows how a key player changes run/pass, concepts, and direction tendencies.

Defensive answer board

Connects offensive prediction to your defensive system, fronts, coverages, checks, and pressures.

Reports

Halftime adjustments, tendency tables, concept usage, and prediction accuracy.
