The role

Not another film library.
Your decision layer.

Built beside your existing film workflow — not as another place to store video.

📊

Charted data in

Use opponent chart data, CSV exports from Hudl or Excel, or manual film entries. Keep your video workflow where it already lives.

🧠

Tendency answers out

See likely concepts, run/pass/RPO splits, star-player alerts, situational tendencies, and defensive recommendations — all from your own data.

🏈

Game-night simple

Box Mode for the press box, Sideline Mode for the field. Both stay live-synced. One coach logs it, the other sees it instantly.

📡

Works offline too

Bad stadium WiFi? No problem. The app falls back to local storage automatically and keeps working. Syncs when signal returns.

⚡

Updates every play

Log a play and the prediction board updates in under a second. As the game unfolds, live tendency carries more weight than film.

📋

Halftime reports

One tap generates a halftime adjustment sheet with split data, top calls, explosive play risk, and drive-by-drive patterns.

Inside the platform

One clean workflow
from prep to kickoff.

Everything in one place — no jumping between apps.

1

Set up your opponent

Name the opponent, set your defensive system, and activate the concepts they actually run.

2

Import your film chart

Drop a Hudl or Excel CSV. The app auto-detects columns and lets you fix the mapping before importing.

3

Review tendency analytics

See concept usage, run/pass/RPO splits, down-distance tendencies, and explosive risk before the game.

4

Open Game Mode

Box coach sets down, distance, formation. The prediction board updates instantly. Sideline sees it live.

5

Log plays on the sideline

Tap Run, Pass, RPO, or Screen after each play. The live log builds your halftime adjustment sheet automatically.

6

Pull the halftime sheet

One tap copies the full halftime adjustment report — splits, top calls, alerts, and explosive play data.
